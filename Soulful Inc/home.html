Our Story

Soulful Fingers .INC was started in the year 2018 by an anonymous upcoming writer who was tired of finding a publisher to read his story and give him a platform to showcase his work. With the help of few young business enthusiasts and a financer he set up a public limited company to fund his own book which sold about 20,000 copies in its first run. Only with in 16 months, Soulful Fingers introduced 20 new authors to the world of writing out of which most of them turned to be successful.
